Spinach and Ricotta Cannelloni

Description

Cannelloni is a delicious italian dish. It consists of stuffed tube shaped pasta in sauce, that is covered with cheese.

One of my favorite meals!

Ingredients

  • cream sauce
  • 250g frozen spinach
  • 500g ricotta
  • 1/3 cup grate parmesan
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • 18-22 dried cannelloni tubes
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella

Steps

Filling

  1. Heat up spinach
  2. Mix spinach with ricotta, parmesan and garlic
  3. Add salt and pepper according to taste

Assemble & Bake

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Assemble cannelloni tubes in a baking pan
  3. Stuff cannelloni tubes with the filling
  4. Pour the sauce over the cannelloni
  5. Scatter cheese, so that everything is covered
  6. Bake for 30 minutes
  7. The cannelloni are now ready to serve
